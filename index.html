About AMCAAJ

At AMCAAJ INTERNATIONAL RECRUITMENT AGENCY INC., we excel in providing top-tier manpower and recruitment solutions. Our core strength lies in our exceptional human resources, focused exclusively on placing highly skilled and trained workers in overseas positions.

Since our establishment in 2005, we have discovered our niche in various countries across the globe, including the GCC Region, Asia Pacific, Europe, Canada, and Australia. Navigating the intricacies of international employment has not been without its challenges. However, our unwavering commitment to addressing these challenges has led to the gradual recognition of our presence.

As we've climbed the ladder of success, we've occasionally ventured close to the precipice. Yet, our dedication to quality over quantity has not wavered. This approach has strengthened our foothold in the industry, earning us a reputation as a trusted and dependable provider of top-tier human resources.

Our track record speaks volumes about our commitment, reliability, and integrity. Our employers and the workers we've deployed stand as living testaments to our exceptional service.

Credentials

We are a licensed agency authorized by the Department of Migrant Workers (DMW), with a mandate to recruit and deploy applicants.
